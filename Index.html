About & Resume

Hello and welcome to my portfolio!

I'm a game designer with six years of extensive knowledge in the use of the Unreal Engine from my time in college. In college, I created two games in Unreal; one which was a first person horror game and the other was a 2D one-button sidescroller. As part of my college experience, I had to undertake work placement so I chose to work at Nebula Interactive. Here, I gained experience as a Junior Level Designer and Game Tester.

Currently, I am working on a personal project in Unreal Engine 5. The project is Fractured Illusions, which I developed for college and I am now remaking it in its original vision.
